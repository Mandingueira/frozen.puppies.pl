About us:

You are more than welcome in our Canine Frozen Semen Center – Frozen Puppies – in Gdansk in Poland.
Our services are provided by a Veterinarian who has gained experience in Canine Reproduction Service - Trade Genetics in Ireland.

Our service:

  • Canine semen collection and analysis;
  • Canine semen freezing;
  • Preparation of chilled semen;
  • Storage of canine frozen semen;
  • Artificial insemination;
  • Vaginal smear of bitches;
  • Export and import of canine frozen and chilled semen - we will help you with everything.

Does not matter where do you live, we will help you to organize export or import of canine frozen or chilled semen !!!

We provide our services in our Centre Frozen Puppies or we can meet you and your pet wherever you need !!!

Our service:

IMPORTANT: A bitch is season is required during semen collection!!!

  • Canine semen collection and its analysis without freezing - semen is collected and evaluated macro and microscopically in our laboratory, this test is used to assess dog's fertility;
  • Preparation of canine chilled semen - semen is collected and evaluated macro and microscopically in our laboratory, this study serves to assess the dog's fertility, then it is preserved using a special diluent/extender that ensures sperm survival to 4-5 days - depending on the quality of the dog's semen;
  • Canine semen freezing - semen is collected and evaluated macro and microscopically in our laboratory, then prepared for freezing with the dry ice method, then it is preserved using a special diluter/extender, portioned into cryovials, depending on the quality of the semen, a single collection can give 1-6 insemination units; we store frozen semen in ours center in liquid nitrogen;
  • Storage of canine frozen semen in liquid nitrogen - in our center you can store canine frozen semen collected in our place or frozen semen from another sperm bank;
  • Artificial insemination of bitches with fresh semen - semen is collected and evaluated macro and microscopically in our laboratory, and then we carry out an artificial insemination;
  • Renting a tank for the transport of canine frozen semen;
  • Vaginal smears in bitches – determination of the sexual cycle; confirmation of proper breeding; detection of silent estrus;
  • Export/import of canine frozen and chilled semen;
  • Issuing a health certificate for the sire.
